This paper examines the growing threat of QR code phishing (QRishing) attacks and analyzes how attackers exploit QR codes to bypass traditional security awareness mechanisms. The study discusses attack techniques, user vulnerabilities, and mitigation strategies to improve cybersecurity resilience.
