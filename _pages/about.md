I am **Henrietta Boowuo**, a Ph.D. Candidate at Purdue University (School of Applied and Creative Computing), whose research focuses on **AI Ethics and Governance**, **Human-Centered AI**, and **Educational Technology**. My work examines fairness, accountability, accessibility, and the societal impact of AI systems, particularly in higher education and digital learning environments. I am especially interested in AI fairness, Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), responsible AI governance, and the development of trustworthy technologies that promote equitable and inclusive user experiences.

My current research explores fairness in Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) systems, responsible AI governance, AI accessibility, and users' experiences interacting with AI-enabled technologies. Through this work, I seek to contribute to the development of AI systems that are equitable, trustworthy, and aligned with human values.

## Research Interests

* AI Ethics and Governance
* Human-Centered AI
* Responsible and Trustworthy AI
* AI Fairness and Accessibility
* Educational Technology
* AI in Higher Education
* Algorithmic Accountability
